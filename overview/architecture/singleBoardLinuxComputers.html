Inexpensive Single Board Wireless Linux Computers

These linux computers such as the Raspberry Pi 3 and BeagleBone Black Wireless are

  • low cost (~400SEK)
  • powerful (1GHz Clock 512MB RAM)
  • small form factor computers (85mm x 55mm)
  • versatile (up to 90 GPIO pins, USB and Etherenet ports)

These powerful computers can be deployed almost anywhere as low cost, smart state machines.

  • They consume on the order of 1 Watt of power
  • The built in wireless features (2.4GHz 802.11n wireless and Bluetooth) eliminates the need for complex control cable plants.
