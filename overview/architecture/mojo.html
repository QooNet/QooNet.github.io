Mojo V3 Embedded Micro

For low-latency applications

  • we pair a BeagleBone Black Wireless
  • with a MOJO Embedded Micro FPGA development board

The entire purpose of the Mojo

  • is to make getting started with FPGA
  • and digital design as easy as possible

The Mojo features include

  • Spartan 6 XC6SLX9 FPGA
  • 84 digital IO pins
  • A microcontroller (ATmega32U4) used for configuring the FPGA, USB communications, and reading the analog pins
