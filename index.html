YggdrasilView - An Open Source IoT Control System

YggdrasilView is an open source control platform optimized for rapid development.

YggdrasilView is

  • Inexpensive and quickly deployable
  • A small physical footprint
  • Easy to use and debug so that the engineer can develop on her own without the assistance of experts.
  • Easily scaleable from small projects to large interconnected projects.
  • Open and text based so that version history control is easy and encouraged.
  • Easy to share and collaborate.
