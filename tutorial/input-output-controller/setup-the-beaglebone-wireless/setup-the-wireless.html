Setup the wireless

  • From the prompt on the BBBW start the wireless configuration app by entering 
    sudo connmanctl
  • The BBBW will now display the connmanctl> prompt. Enter: 
    scan wifi
services

    You will see a list of wifi networks available. On each line you will see the name of the wifi network and next to it the service name in the form of wifi_xxx_yyy_managed_psk 

    agent on
connect wifi_xxx_yyy_managed_psk

    where wifi_xxx_yyy_managed_psk is the name of the service that corresponds to your network. You will be prompted for a pass-phase for your network. You will be notified if the connection is successful

    Exit the connmanctl menu

    quit
  • Check the network connection by typing 
    ifconfig -a

    you should see a valid ip address under wlan0. Now you can remove the micro-USB cable and power the BBBW with a 5 V power supply or you can continue to power the BBBW with the micro-USB.

  • Log into the BBBW over the wireless 
    ssh debian@<hostname>.local

    where <hostname> is the hostname you setup in the previous step. You can also use the ip address of wlan0 in place of <hostname>.local
