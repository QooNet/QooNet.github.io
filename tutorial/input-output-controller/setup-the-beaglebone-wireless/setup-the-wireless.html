Setup the wireless

  • From the prompt on the BBBW start the wireless configuration app by entering
    • $ sudo connmanctl
    • The BBBW will now display the connmanctl> prompt
      • connmanctl> scan wifi
      • connmanctl> services
        • You will see a list of wifi networks available
        • On each line you will see the name of the wifi network and next to it the service name in the form of wifi_xxx_yyy_managed_psk
      • connmanctl> agent on
      • connmanctl> connect wifi_xxx_yyy_managed_psk
        • where wifi_xxx_yyy_managed_psk is the name of the service that corresponds to your network
        • you will be prompted for a pass-phase for your network
        • you will be notified if the connection is successful
      • connmanctl> quit
  • Check the network connection by typing
    • $ ifconfig -a
    • you should see a valid ip address under wlan0
  • Now you can remove the micro-USB cable and power the BBBW with a 5 V power supply
    • Or you can continue to power the BBBW with the micro-USB
  • Log into the BBBW over the wireless
    • $ ssh debian@<hostname>.local
    • where <hostname> is the hostname you setup in the previous step.
    • you can also use the ip address of wlan0 in place of <hostname>.local
